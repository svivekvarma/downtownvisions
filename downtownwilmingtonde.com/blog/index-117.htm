Ladybug Festival

Marty Hageman
July 9, 2015
Entertainment, Events

The Ladybug Music Festival attracts some of the top talent from our region and brings it all to Downtown Wilmington for a night of music and celebration.

The 2015 Ladybug Music Festival is shaping up to be one of the best!

I encourage you to bring your family and friends to this great event on Thursday, July 16 to experience a wide variety of musical styles while enjoying all that Downtown Wilmington has to offer from our restaurants, stores, and street vendors.

I hope to see you there.

Marty

Reggae in the Square

Marty Hageman
July 6, 2015
Events, Entertainment

Downtown Wilmington is the place to be for all music styles. We've seen Rock, Jazz, Blues, Hip-Hop, Rockabily, Folk, and dozens of other great musical styles played throughout our downtown.

This Saturday, July 11, some talented Reggae artists will be performing on Rodney Square - we'll get to hear from artists like Lex A Million, Maine Gwap, Shane Koosong, and Jah Torius.

The concert is free and open to the public.

I hope to see you there!

Marty

You don't want to miss Thursday's block party

Marty Hageman
June 23, 2015
Entertainment, Events

This Thursday's Block Party - Riverfront Rumpus - is an event you don't want to miss.

I'm just going to quote from the INWilmington site:

Rocky Bluewinkle will be stopping by (5-5:30pm) to join IN the fun with hands-on activities provided by Cute Poop, The Creative Vision Factory, Delaware Center for the Contemporary Arts, Delaware Children's Museum, Delaware Historical Society, Delaware Nature Society, Hagley, The Music School of Delaware and Sycamore Hill Church! 

The Babied Family,  Bret Smith Gems, Mirrored Image Photography and Kristin's Kreations will also be on site with delightful products to browse!
PLUS
Enjoy mainstage musical performances by Old Baltimore Speedway, RKVC and Arts Academy at The Grand's Saxophone Students of Harry Spencer! Grab a bite to eat from places like Molly's Ice Cream Cafe, Drop Squad Kitchen, Ubon Thai Cuisine, River Rock Kitchen and Timothy's Riverfront Grill, sip on a cocktail at the Veritas Wine & Spirits bar and browse local artists and vendors as you soak up the sun and take IN all of the great music!
- See more at: http://inwilmingtonde.com/events/RiverfrontRumpus#sthash.Gis8kHH2.dpuf

Great music, food, and fun - Come out to our Riverfront for a great time this Thursday!

Marty

Clifford Brown Jazz Festival

Marty Hageman
June 9, 2015
Art, Entertainment, Events

The Clifford Brown Jazz Festival is a Downtown Wilmington tradition going back more than 25 years.

This festival has grown in prominence and scope, with performances and activities scheduled throughout the week and throughout the downtown area. You can click here for a complete line up of events.

This year's line up of artists is truly impressive - from John Sax Williams to Pieces of a Dream to the Tito Puente, Jr. Orchestra - you don't want to miss this year's festival!

I'm proud to say Downtown Visions will once again be providing help and support to this great festival that draws thousands of people to our city from around the country and around the world.

I hope to see you at the festival!

Marty
